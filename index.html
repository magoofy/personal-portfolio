About Me

Hello! My name is Siosaia Akau, but I go by Afi (off-ee). I was born and raised in West Point, Utah where I spent the first 19 years of my life. I attended Syracuse High while also attending Weber State University, and by the end of my high school career, I recieved my associates degree. I am trying to learn to cook (ever so slowly) and code at Berkley's Bootcamp!
